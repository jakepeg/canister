# Pricing and Retention Policy

## Plan Commitments

- `Essential (Free)`
  - Price: `$0`
  - Limits: message only, `200` characters max
  - Files: not included
  - Unlock horizon: up to `1` year from creation
- `Signature`
  - Price: `$12` one-time
  - Limits: up to `5` files, `5MB` each, `25MB` total
  - Unlock horizon: up to `5` years from creation
  - Post-unlock availability: `30` days, then access expires
- `Legacy`
  - Price: `$39` one-time
  - Limits: up to `10` files, `10MB` each, `100MB` total
  - Unlock horizon: up to `50` years from creation
  - Retention: long-horizon target

## Continuity Disclaimer

Use this exact language in plan copy where space allows:

`Lifetime retention goal, subject to network availability and protocol continuity.`

## Deletion and Retention Rules

- Signature capsules continue to enforce unlock-date access.
- After unlock, Signature content remains available for 30 days.
- After that window, Signature content access is denied by policy.
- Legacy is retained for long-horizon access and can introduce optional user-initiated delete controls later.

## Margin Guardrails

- Pricing decisions must satisfy stress-case margin.
- New limits/prices are blocked if estimated gross margin falls below `55%`.
- Margin model must include:
  - ICP storage reserve,
  - payment fees,
  - operational reserve,
  - support burden.
